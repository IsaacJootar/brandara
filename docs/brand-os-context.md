# Brandara — Product Context

## What this product is

Brandara is an AI-powered personal brand and content intelligence platform.
It helps B2B founders, consultants, and agencies create social media content,
schedule and publish it, engage their audience, track warm leads, and monitor
how their brand appears in AI-generated answers — all in one place.

## Who it is for

- B2B founders building a personal brand on LinkedIn and X
- Independent consultants and coaches growing their authority
- Social media agencies managing 5–30 client brands
- African SMBs using social media and WhatsApp to sell

## Where it is built for

Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa — then expanding globally.
The product is deeply African-market-first: local payment methods,
local tone modes, local holiday campaign packs, WhatsApp marketing,
and African-context AI visibility monitoring.

## The three philosophies

1. **We stand on strong shoulders** — Brandara inherits the best from
   Blotato (affordable all-in-one), Buffer (clean scheduling UX),
   Ordinal (engagement automation), Hootsuite (agency workspace),
   and Plot.so (intelligence beyond scheduling). Then adds what nobody else has.

2. **Simple is the hardest thing to build** — every button, label,
   and message must be understood by a non-technical Nigerian business
   owner with zero training. No jargon ever.

3. **Writing is the engine, not a feature** — content creation is why
   users open Brandara every day. Everything else serves that loop.

## Content generation — 3 variations always

Every generation produces 3 variations:
- **Authority angle:** expert positioning, teach-first
- **Story angle:** narrative, client result, warm and human
- **Bold/Opinion angle:** strong take, drives engagement and sharing

Each variation is generated for every selected platform simultaneously,
adapted for that platform's format, tone, and algorithm.

## Platforms — 7 total

LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Facebook, Instagram, Threads — full publish and schedule.
WhatsApp — copy generation only (broadcast, status, promo texts). Manual send in v1.
TikTok — text toolkit only (caption, script, hashtags, bio). No API in v1.

## Payments — Africa only

Flutterwave (all other African markets — 30+ currencies)
No Stripe.
